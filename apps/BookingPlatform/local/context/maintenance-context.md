# BookingPlatform Maintenance Context

This role manages the upkeep of an **outdoor bowls** facility.

Manage all tasks; view, assign, accept, work and close
Can make bookings
Notify admin when work is complete
View all tasks on a facility
View tasks assigned to self

## Typical Maintenance Task Examples
- Uneven or damaged green surface (rink surface category)
- Damaged or missing ditch boards
- Boundary marker or rink number post repair
- Bowls mat worn or missing
- Scoreboard repair or replacement
- Shelter / pavilion maintenance (roof, guttering, seating)
- Floodlight fault (where the club has floodlit greens)
- Path or car park hazard
- Equipment storage issue (locker, cabinet, bowls trolley)
- General grounds upkeep (hedges, path edging, drainage)
